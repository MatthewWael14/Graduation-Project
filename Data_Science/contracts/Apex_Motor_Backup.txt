MASTER SUPPLY AND SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Document Reference: SLA-APEXMOTOR-2026-V1
Effective Date: June 21, 2026

PARTIES
This agreement is entered into by and between Global Manufacturing Inc. (hereinafter "Buyer") 
and Apex Motor Backup (hereinafter "Supplier").

DELIVERY AND LEAD TIMES
The Supplier agrees to fulfill all standard purchase orders within a strict maximum turnaround 
window. The standard delivery SLA lead time is established at exactly one hundred twenty (120) hours 
from the moment the electronic purchase order is acknowledged by the Supplier's ERP system.

QUANTITY AND CAPACITY COMMITMENTS
The Supplier commits to maintaining a dedicated backup inventory capacity of exactly five hundred (500) 
units of Electric Motor Rotor to satisfy emergency orders issued by the Buyer.

UNIT PRICING AND COSTS
All purchases under this agreement shall be billed at a fixed rate of exactly forty-five dollars and 
zero cents ($45.00) USD per unit of Electric Motor Rotor.

LIQUIDATED DAMAGES FOR DELAY
Time is of the essence. In the event that the Supplier fails to deliver the materials within the 
aforementioned 120-hour window, the Supplier shall be liable for a delay penalty. The Buyer shall 
deduct a sum of $250.00 USD for each full calendar day the shipment is delayed beyond the 
agreed delivery date. Furthermore, for any individual units missing from the manifest upon 
arrival (short-shipments), a missed item penalty of $100.00 USD per missing unit shall be 
applied.

QUALITY ASSURANCE AND DEFECTS
The Supplier warrants that all raw materials will meet the strict engineering tolerance standards 
outlined in Appendix A. The minimum acceptable quality threshold is ninety-eight percent 
(98.0%) defect-free yield per batch. Should the quality score fall below this threshold, a quality 
penalty rate equal to fifteen percent (15%) of the total order value will be withheld by the 
Buyer.
